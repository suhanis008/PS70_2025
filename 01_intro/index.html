The idea would be to program a set of specific movements, such as making a fold, lifting the paper, or creasing, and then combine them to build different models. Since every model is essentially a sequence of folds, the robot could replicate them step by step. This project would let me integrate robotics with origami while exploring how to design arm mobility for precise movements. Origami models can be grouped into categories, <a href="https://origami-resource-center.com/origami-classification/">like these</a> , based on the complexity of their folds.
