At first, I wanted to make a hand that would rise and fall through the hole in the box. But during testing, I realized that the hand often slipped all the way through, which caused the sculpture to fail. <p class = "margin"></p> <img src="./Hand image.png" alt="generic placeholder image" width = "400" height = "400"> <p class = "margin"></p> To fix this, I replaced the hand with a sign. The sign only moves up and down without disappearing into the box, which solved the problem.
